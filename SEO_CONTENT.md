## Social Media Sharing (Open Graph)
To ensure articles appear correctly on WhatsApp, Facebook, and Twitter:

1.  **Image:** Use a 1200x630px image for each article.
2.  **Title:** Keep under 60 characters to avoid truncation.
3.  **Description:** Keep under 160 characters.
4.  **Implementation:** Use `react-helmet-async` in your React components to dynamically update these tags for each article.
